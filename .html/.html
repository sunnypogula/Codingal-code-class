Welcome to Telugu Kala Samithi

Telugu Kala Samithi (TKS) is a social & cultural entity in the Kingdom of Bahrain established to serve the underprivileged and the Telugu expatriate community.

We assist with social services, labor disputes, repatriation, cultural programmes, sports and more. We collaborate with the Indian Embassy and local ministries to support those in need.
